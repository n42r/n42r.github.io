I see that software and technology have become so complex that we need new approaches to tackle them. 

I'm a life-long tinkerer, software architect/engineer, and researcher with many achievements under my belt. However, where I shine most is on the intersection of engineering with product, people, and cross-functional leadership.

I started out in robotics/AI and game development (Crystal Space, anyone?) and then moved on to the intersection of R&D, product development, and leadership: first in mobile app development and then in data management. I just completed a PhD where I proposed a novel [architectural pattern](phd) for cross-organizational data cataloguing (related: data mesh, data lake).

Having lived and worked in three continents has led me to become multicultural, resilient, and highly adaptable. Personally, I'm a laid-back person with a fine-tuned critical apparatus and a unique combination of passion/beginner-mindset on one side and strategic vision on the other.
