I have a passion for envisioning, anticipating, and managing technical products with a focus on simplicity, strategy, and innovation.

Throughout my career, I have engaged in a variety of impactful projects spanning different domains. I have successfully managed the development of a card-making social media app, developed AI players for games, and played a key role in the founding and development of a team to compete in the international RoboCup AI soccer competition. 

In recent years, I have led the development of a data management software suite for an EU computer-aided manufacturing project, involving companies such as Airbus. My work in this area involved managing the whole product, designing the overarching architecture-including interfaces and data formats-and doing the research. This led to [my PhD](https://n42r.github.io/phd) in which I propose a novel data architecture pattern akin to data maesh, data spaces, and data lakes. In the past, I conducted research in symbolic AI, robotics, and cognitive science and published papers in top conferences.

When it comes to managing innovative products, adopting powerful tools can be a competitive advantage. Development frameworks and the latest X-as-a-service template are tools, but cutting-edge approaches (ex., conceptual, scientific, legal) are also valuable tools. My specialization lies in the latter category.

With a background in engineering and computer science and a proven track record in product management, leadership, and research, I offer a distinct blend of technical expertise and strategic thinking. I thrive in interdisciplinary environments, collaborating with cross-functional teams to drive innovation and deliver successful projects.
