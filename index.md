I have a passion for envisioning, strategizing, and managing innovative technical products. My specializataion in the last 5 years has been in data architecture, proposing a kind of a 'next-gen' of the likes of data mesh, data space, data lake. However, my experience is not limited to technically innovative products. I have managed B2C products in the past with a focus on usability. 

Throughout my career, I have engaged in a variety of impactful projects spanning different domains. I have successfully managed the development of a card-making social media app, developed AI players for games, and played a key role in the founding and development of a team to compete in the international RoboCup AI soccer robotics competition.

My work in this area involved managing the whole product, designing the overarching data architecture-including interfaces and data formats-and overseeing its implementation. This led to [my PhD](https://n42r.github.io/phd) in which I propose a novel data architecture pattern.

With a background in engineering and computer science and a proven track record in product management, leadership, and R&D, I offer a distinct blend of technical expertise and strategic thinking. Having lived and worked in three continents, I have the ability to effectively communicate, collaborate, and thrive in multicultural environments.
