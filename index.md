I have a passion for envisioning, anticipating, and creating technical products with a focus on simplicity.

Throughout my career, I have engaged in a variety of impactful projects spanning different domains. I have successfully managed the development of a card-making social media app, developed AI players for games, and played a key role in the founding and development of a team for the international RoboCup soccer simulation competition. 

In recent years, I have led the development of a data management software suite for an EU computer-aided manufacturing project, involving companies such as Airbus. My work in this area involved identifying domain requirements, designing the overarching architecture, and supervising a team of developers (this led to [my PhD](https://n42r.github.io/phd) in data architecture). In the past, I conducted research in symbolic AI, robotics, and cognitive science and published papers in top conferences.

Technology such as frameworks, stacks, X-as-a-service are tools, but cutting-edge approaches-scientific, legal, conceptual-can sometimes be more valuable tools. With a background in engineering and computer science and a proven track record in product development, leadership, and research, I offer a distinct blend of technical expertise and strategic thinking. I thrive in interdisciplinary environments, collaborating with cross-functional teams to drive innovation and deliver successful projects.
