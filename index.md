I like to imagine, look forward, and design innovative products, with an eye out on simplicity. Typically these products have had the form of software, but not exclusively, for example, I used to build sound systems as a hobby at some point. 

When building innovative products, sometimes adopting emerging tools is an advantage. Technology is a tool, but complex and emerging scientific approaches are also "tools." Thanks to my strong academic background, when there is a promising lead, I do not hesitate in wearing the R&D goggles and diving in search of opportunities.

Throughout my career, I have engaged in a variety of impactful projects spanning different domains. I have successfully developed a 3D computer board game in my undergraduate study, contributed to improving AI players in a mobile game, and played a key role in the founding and development of a team for the international RoboCup soccer simulation competition. Additionally, I have led the development of a data management software suite for a computer-aided manufacturing project, collaborating with renowned companies. My work in this area involved understanding domain-specific requirements, designing an overarching architecture, and supervising a team of developers (this work eventually led to my PhD). Furthermore, I have conducted research in symbolic Artificial Intelligence, robotics, and cognitive science, resulting in the publication of papers in top AI conferences.

With a background in engineering and computer science and a proven track record in product development, leadership, and research, I offer a distinct blend of technical expertise and strategic thinking. I thrive in interdisciplinary environments, collaborating with cross-functional teams to drive innovation and deliver successful projects. I am actively seeking opportunities to leverage my skills and experience in a role that combines technical excellence, product development, and leadership, while fostering a collaborative and dynamic work environment (See my [résumé](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/n42r/n42r.github.io/main/assets/pdf/kasrin-resume.pdf) for more information).
